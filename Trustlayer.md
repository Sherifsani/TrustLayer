# **TrustLayer**: AI-Powered Credit Intelligence & Fraud Risk   Infrastructure for Africa 

**Problem**  
Nigeria’s digital economy lacks a reliable trust infrastructure. Businesses struggle with fake identities, loan defaults, forged documents, and fraud risks. At the same time, millions of financially active individuals remain excluded from credit due to lack of traditional credit history or their credit score sucks.  
**Solution**  
TrustLayer is a B2C \+ B2B trust intelligence platform that combines identity verification, telecom behaviour analysis, financial data insights, AI fraud detection, and consent-based data sharing to generate real-time Trust Scores and Fraud Risk Scores. Businesses verify users before offering financial services, employment, insurance, rentals, or digital access.   
**How TrustLayer Works:**   
• AI driven KYC using NIN, BVN, facial verification & device intelligence   
• Telecom behavioural analysis (SIM age, recharge patterns, device stability)   
• Financial behaviour monitoring via open banking integrations   
• AI document authentication detecting manipulated PDFs and forged statements   
• Real time trust monitoring and continuous fraud risk assessment   
• Consent Graph allowing users control over data access and sharing.  
**Squad APIs Used:**  Payment Collection API – individuals/ businesses pay per verification, fraud check, and subscription.

| Pillars | How TrustLayer addresses it |
| :---- | :---- |
| AI Automation  | Automates identity verification and credit intelligence workflows |
| Squad API’s | Powers monetization, subscriptions and automated financial services. |
| Use of data | Uses telecom, financial and behavioural data to predict risk in real time. |
| Financial Innovation | Introduces consent based trust infrastucture enabling financial inclusion across Africa. |
